ABOUT US
Jai Devi Sunderlal Inter College Located in Gurudeen Kheda JajaMau Fatehpur 84, Unnao. It is a state board school with a team of 12 dedicated and professional faculties to ensure that the children get the most out of their education.

In 2014, Jai Devi Sunderlal U M V Gurudin Khera Jaja Mau Unnao was launched. The primary medium of instruction is Hindi and English.

The school strives to provide the best learning environment. Students from 9 to 10 are taught in this school. The current student strength of the school is around 824. It also has a library with more than 600 books.
The school has given exceptional results in the academic field and its students have also excelled in extra-curricular activities.
