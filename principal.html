PRINCIPAL'S DESK
Dear Friends
Greetings to all
I believe that God continues to create children since he is not yet tired of man. I also believe that man is yet to understand the mind of a child. I believe in striving for excellence in everything but also believe that excellence alone is not everything. I believe children must aim for high grades but I also believe that children who score low grades also succeed in life. I believe in grades that help children pass an exam but I also believe in values which see them through life. I believe parents when they tell me that children do not like to study but I also believe in children who tell me parents do not have time for them.I believe in destiny but I also believe that we are makers of it. Our aim is to give your child the best possible start to life in school and meet the needs of every child entrusted to our care. We are a school with a difference where children learn a lot and enjoy coming to school. We will strive hard to make the best possible efforts to inculcate strong values and amalgam of scholastic and co-scholastic activities.

Thank you
