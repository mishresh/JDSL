ABOUT US
Jai Devi Sunderlal Inter College Located in Gurudeen Kheda JajaMau Fatehpur 84, Unnao. It is a state board school with a team of 12 dedicated and professional faculties to ensure that the children get the most out of their education. In 2014, Jai Devi Sunderlal U M V Gurudin Khera Jaja Mau Unnao was launched. The primary medium of instruction is Hindi and . The school strives to provide the best learning environment. Students from 9 to 10 are taught in this school. The current student strength of the school is around 824. It also has a library with more than 600 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic field and its students have also excelled in extra-curricular activities. . . . . . .

Principal's Desk
Dear Friends
I believe that God continues to create children since he is not yet tired of man. I also believe that man is yet to understand the mind of a child. I believe in striving for excellence in everything but also believe that excellence alone is not everything. I believe children must aim for high grades but I also believe that children who score low grades also succeed in life. I believe in grades that help children pass an exam but I also believe in values which see them through life. I believe parents when they tell me that children do not like to study but I also believe in children who tell me parents do not have time for them. . . . .
