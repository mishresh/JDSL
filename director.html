DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE
DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE
Dear Friends
Greetings to all
Welcome to J.D.S.L, INTER COLLEGE, we are committed to making sure that our mission to provide world class education remains uncompromised. Over the years, the campus has emerged as a green oasis in the midst of an increasingly environment. Our vast playing fields and the range of our scientifically designed Playground equipment has set standards for other schools to follow. College is affiliated from State board, and our trademark is to emparting best in class education with highest results. There is Co-ed. education system and whole college is in WIFI zone and under CCTV. Surveillance to ensure safety for all. We have Geography lab and a plan to make a seperate Laboratories for Science and Mathematics so that our students engage in hands-on education. We have a good collection of Academics and other books in the Library so students can groom up with ocean of knowledge and update too. Our emphasis on activity-based learning and public speaking has enabled our students to become confident and creative leaders in society. Our dedicated and affectionate teachers have made this institution a ‘home away from home’ for generations of children. Our students are high achievers in the Board Examinations, as well as in the various Government and competitive examinations like IITJEE, NEET, SSC, AIR FORCE, TEACHER, DEFENCE and others. Our alumni make us proud in a variety of professions in India. We are proud of the ongoing and past achievements of our students and alumni. In these times of uncertainty and distress, We are following the proper protocol and guidelines of Government. We continue to work tirelessly to support all the educational needs of our students.

Thank you
